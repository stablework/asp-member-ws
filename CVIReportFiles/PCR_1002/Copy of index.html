The foundational difference is found in what an assessment measures

At the core of our technology is the assessment you just took called the Core Values Index.

It characterizes and quantifies what Abraham Maslow called the unchanging innate nature of a person that inscribes where a person can make their highest and most productive contribution to the world.

The industry standard metric used to measure wether an assessment changes over time is repeat score reliability. The CVI has proven in longetudinal studies to have the highest at over 94% year after year. It provides a clear and useful picture of the innate, unchanging nature of an individual. This is much different then personality and behavioral assessments.

How can it be so accurate and take only 10 minutes?

It’s the only human assessment that uses strategic and tactical values in a forced choice format, to gain unequivocal insight into the innate nature of the person. Behavioral and psychological tools are by their nature fraught with problems of misrepresentation by the users, who tend to create false positives when completing the respective instruments.

This is made possible by the construct of the instruments themselves and the use of context loaded questions. It is also encouraged by the common requirement to disclose negative, or perceived to be negative information about one’s self. Since the CVI is comprised of only positive values, no such negative disclosure is required, and no context is provided.

We have the only human assessment process that provides 94% repeat score reliability, year after year. Other assessments typically provide reliability between 60% and 80%. This is several orders of magnitude improvement—attesting to the fact that we are capturing the innate unchanging nature of people.

This is why the CVI is the only assessment that should be used as a pre-screening tool for employment application processes.

How We Create a Top Performer Profile

The usual subjective process of hiring new employees includes reading through resumes, performing phone interviews, conducting endless personal interviews, and gambling on the hope that the new candidate will be similar to your current top performers.

However, the Top Performer Profile (TPP) is the first Employment Pre-Selection tool that acts as a true objective screen at the very beginning of the hiring process. The TPP™ is based on each company’s current top performers’ outputs and profits, and it is used to pre-select future top performers from incoming candidates.

Steps to creating a Top Performer Profile:

Rate Current Players | Have client grade current team in the position rating each person on measured performance as an A,B,C, or D player.

Everyone Takes the CVI | We have everyone currently in that position take the Core Values Index (CVI)

We Create A Specific Profile for the Position | We interview our client to discover the actual tasks and functions of the position.

We have the only medical grade image processing algorithms applied to a noisy database of core values scores that allow us to deduce the core value profile of top performers in a given position, in a given company, at any given time. This creates the finished Top Performer Profile (TPP) for our clients – A tight range of score of 10 measurement points which include the four core values and six types.

The profile screens out over 90% of people who aren’t wired to actually do the tasks of the position while screening in those who are wired to be top performers.

It’s not just for hiring more people

Having TPP’s for every position in a company enables the executive team to compare everyone in the organization to every position. This gives accurate insight into where to move people around within the organization enabling good people that are underperforming to find the right role where they instantly become Top Performers. You have a real time audit always in place making sure everyone is positioned to do their best work that matches who they are.

Implementation is simple and causes no distractions

We don’t change our clients hiring methods, instead we change who hiring managers allow to be interviewed. We ensure only people wired to be highly productive in the tasks of your companies specific positions are selected for interviews.

This actually immediately eliminates a lot of time and effort that used to be spent sifting through applications and resume’s in the old subjective process in deciding who to interview.

How Our Clients Use It:

Clients attract candidates who are compared to the Top Performer Profile | The client then advertises for positions as they normally do, and those who submit a resume are sent the CVI. All candidates and their scores automatically show up in our website, that only our clients have access to.
