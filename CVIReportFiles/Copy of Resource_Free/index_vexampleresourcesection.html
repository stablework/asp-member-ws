A resource guide for you as we assist you in finding a position to maximize your contributions and self actualization.

Few things in life are as stressful—or potentially as rewarding—as changing careers. That's why executives, managers, skilled and part-time workers all look to The Resource for career guidance.

In a typical agency, job seekers are rushed in and out the door. We’re different. We partner with individuals and employers through five specialty divisions, each overseen by an expert team of Resource Leaders.

THE RESOURCE SPECIALTY DIVISIONS:

skill
For those roles requiring certification or hands-on experience, such as welders, CNC operators, quality control, maintenance mechanics, forklift operators, specific machine operators and more.
career
For executives, managers, engineers, accountants and HR professionals.
it
For technically qualified individuals such as CIOs, storage architects, network administrators, desktop support technicians and helpdesk analysts.
temp
For a prescreened pool of qualified candidates in general-clerical and light-industrial roles, including temp-to-hire.
idea
For businesses and individuals who need help creating solutions for their greatest challenges.

Our trained and experienced staff takes the time to understand what motivates and drives you. Only then will we match you with the right company, in the right role, at the right time. We do this for one simple reason: your success is our success.

CAREER CONCIERGE PROGRAM
  • Red-carpet treatment for ongoing job seekers' experience and career development
  • For job seekers that are placed; ongoing follow-ups to ensure matches remain successful
  • For those that aren't placed; coaching to hone skills and re-evaluate career match qualities

The Grass IS Greener

Join the better Work-Life Revolution.

#TEMPLATE/GrassIsGreener/PRIMARY-SECONDARY.tpl#

The secret is not to learn how to have fun and joy at work; the secret is to do work that is naturally engaging to who you are and what you are, so you know that you are making your highest and best contribution. This allows you to experience real joy naturally as part of your work-life. This is what Abraham Maslow called "peak experiences." Sometimes this makes the right job downright fun.

    Inside you will find:
  • How and why people and businesses get into this wrong job predicament. It contains stories and examples and business results that illustrate the cost of putting people in wrong jobs.
  • Stories of people who have spent years in wrong occupations, but now have a job that fits their deepest nature; the difference this has made for these people, for their families and for their employers.
  • We will help you understand what your Real Core Values Nature™ is, and how it affects the way you feel about your job. How does this Real Core Values Self™ affect your ability and willingness to perform the tasks you are now required to perform?
  • We will provide exercises and self-scoring questionnaires that will give you a new understanding of your deepest nature and where you belong in this world.
